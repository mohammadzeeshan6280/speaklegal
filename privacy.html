Our policies for refund

Refund Policy

At SPEAKLEGAL, we strive to provide the highest quality legal services to our clients. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to request a refund. Our refund policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted and the process for requesting them.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

1. Service Cancellation by Client: If you decide to cancel our legal services before any significant work has been performed, you may be eligible for a partial refund. The amount refunded will depend on the extent of work already completed and any expenses incurred.

2. Service Cancellation by Law Firm: If we are unable to provide the agreed-upon services due to unforeseen circumstances on our part, you will be entitled to a full refund of any fees paid.

3. Unsatisfactory Service: If you believe that the quality of our legal services is not up to the agreed-upon standards, please contact us to discuss your concerns. We will review your case and, if appropriate, may offer a partial or full refund.

Non-Refundable Fees

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Initial consultation fees
  • Court filing fees and other third-party expenses
  • Any costs incurred on behalf of the client (e.g., expert witness fees, document retrieval fees)

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Contact Us: Reach out to our office via phone or email to explain the reason for your refund request. Please provide all relevant details, including your case number, the services rendered, and the reason for your dissatisfaction.

2.Decision: Upon receiving your request, our team will review your case and the work performed. We may contact you for additional information or clarification.

3. Unsatisfactory Service: After a thorough review, we will inform you of our decision regarding your refund request. If approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days.
